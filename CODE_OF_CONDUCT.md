# Lumina Community Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Lumina is dedicated to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and safe environment for all community members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to building a community where participation is a harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- **Using welcoming and inclusive language**
- **Focusing on what is best for the community**
- **Showing empathy towards other community members**
- **Being professional and constructive** in all interactions
- **Acknowledging and learning from mistakes**
- **Respecting privacy and confidentiality**, especially regarding patient data discussions

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination based on any protected characteristic
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Condescending or patronizing language
- Repeated interruption or talking over others
- Sharing confidential information
- Any conduct that violates laws or regulations

---

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Direct Communication** (if safe): Politely address the behavior with the person involved
2. **Report to Maintainers**: Email [project-email@example.com] with:
   - Description of the incident
   - Participants involved
   - Witnesses (if any)
   - Context and timeline
   - Any evidence or documentation
3. **Anonymously**: Use GitHub's private reporting features or contact a maintainer confidentially

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment** (within 48 hours): Confirm receipt of the report
2. **Investigation** (within 1 week): Gather information from all parties
3. **Decision** (within 2 weeks): Determine appropriate action
4. **Appeal** (within 30 days): Process for contesting decisions

### Consequences

Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include:

- **Warning**: Formal notice of violation with expectations for change
- **Temporary Ban**: Suspension from community spaces for a defined period (1-3 months)
- **Permanent Ban**: Removal from the community and all associated spaces
- **Legal Action**: If behavior violates laws

The severity of consequences matches the severity and pattern of behavior.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues, discussions, and pull requests
- Project repositories and forks
- Official community events and meetings
- Public spaces where someone represents the project
- Private communications if they relate to project conduct

This Code of Conduct does NOT apply to:

- Private discussions unrelated to the project
- Other projects or communities
- Interactions outside of Lumina spaces

---

## Conflicts of Interest

Community leaders must disclose conflicts of interest related to:

- Financial relationships
- Personal relationships
- Employment relationships
- Competing projects

Conflicts will be managed through recusal or transparency, depending on severity.
