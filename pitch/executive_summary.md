# Executive Summary — Lumina

## The Opportunity

Mental health is the largest unaddressed healthcare challenge of our generation. Over 970 million people globally live with a mental health or substance use condition. The treatment gap — the proportion who need care but don't receive it — exceeds 70% in most countries. In Brazil, estimates suggest that fewer than 20% of people with mental health conditions receive adequate treatment.

The problem is not only access. It is quality, continuity, and the capacity of clinical systems to function effectively under the pressure they face.

**Lumina addresses the intelligence gap at the heart of the clinical systems problem.**

---

## The Problem

Mental health care teams operate with fundamentally inadequate information infrastructure:

- **Triage is reactive**: risk is assessed at a single point in time, not tracked continuously
- **Care gaps are invisible**: clinical teams have no real-time view of which patients are deteriorating between appointments
- **Decision support is absent**: clinicians make high-stakes decisions without access to synthesized longitudinal data
- **Coordination is manual**: care continuity across providers depends on email, phone calls, and shared spreadsheets

The result: preventable deterioration, avoidable crises, and significant clinician burnout from administrative overhead.

---

## The Solution

Lumina is an AI-native clinical intelligence platform that gives mental health care teams the information they need to act earlier and care better.

**Three core capabilities:**

1. **Intelligent Triage** — Continuous psychosocial risk scoring from structured assessment data, giving teams a prioritized view of who needs attention and why
2. **Care Journey Intelligence** — Real-time monitoring of care continuity, flagging gaps before they become dropouts
3. **Clinical Decision Support** — AI-generated session preparation briefings and team analytics, structured for clinical utility

**What makes Lumina different:**
- Ethics-first architecture — AI that is explicitly bounded, explainable, and always overridable
- Designed for clinical trust — outputs clinicians can explain to colleagues, supervisors, and patients
- Between-session reach — the only layer of the care system monitoring the space between appointments

---

## Business Model

**Target market**: Digital health platforms, integrated behavioral health organizations, outpatient mental health services

**Revenue model**: B2B SaaS
- Per-seat licensing for care team access
- Volume-based pricing for patient panel size
- Enterprise: custom integration and deployment

**Go-to-market**: Direct sales to digital health platforms and mental health service organizations, with a focus on organizations already operating at scale and seeking to improve clinical quality metrics.

In three years, Lumina is the intelligence layer that powers clinical decision-making across Brazil's largest digital health platforms — processing millions of patient signals per day, reducing the mean time from risk signal to clinical action from weeks to hours, and contributing measurably to reducing the treatment gap for the patients who need care most.
